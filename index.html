WAIST/HEIGHT RATIO:0.6

Your Waist to Height Ration (WHtR) is a measure of abdominal fat accumulation and is calculated by dividing your waist circumference by you height in inches. WHtR has been shown to be one of the most effective predictors of cardiovascular health risk and life expectancy. Your WHtR is not a diagnosis. If your BMI is high due to increased muscle mass, but your WHtR is normal, your BMI may not accurately reflect your overall health.
